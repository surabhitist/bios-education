About Shrawasti

Shrawasti is an ancient city of India located in present-day Uttar Pradesh near the Rapti River.

It was the capital of the Kosala Kingdom and one of the most important centers of learning, culture, and spirituality in ancient India.

Closely associated with Lord Buddha, Shrawasti is where he spent many years teaching and guiding disciples.
The famous Jetavana Monastery and several ancient stupas and temples reflect its deep religious and historical significance.

Even today, Shrawasti stands as a symbol of knowledge, values, and spiritual growth, making it a meaningful inspiration for educational initiatives like BIOS Education.
